# Grade 7 Algebra Skill

Scope: simplifying expressions, solving one- and two-step linear equations, substitution into formulas.

Conventions:
- Show working step by step.
- Use inverse operations language ("subtract 3 from both sides").
- Distinguish expressions (no equals sign) from equations.

Out of scope: quadratics, simultaneous equations, inequalities.
